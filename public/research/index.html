Research

Data-Driven Analysis and Forecasting for Port Throughout

Professor Andrew in his Implementation Science & Engineering Lab (ISELab), where I can earn solid hands-on experiences with enormous volumes of real-world data here. Since Aug. 2022, I have been working as a research assistant in his esteemed lab. I am responsible for conducting a cost-effectiveness analysis for the current project, “Balanced Opioid Initiative”. This project concerns Opioid abuse, which is associated with a significant increase in overdose deaths in recent years. Public health data indicate that approximately half of all opioid medications are prescribed in primary care. With a focus on this issue, this project’s main objective is to guide decision-makers on implementation strategies to improve opioid prescribing practices in primary care.
